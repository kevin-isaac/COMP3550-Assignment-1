Water

Water is an all too necessary commodity to be in possession of when it comes to our survival. All living organisms need water on some timely basis in order to continue living. So it would make sense that an abundant supply of water would be all that we needed to live happily as far as our water consumption goes. However, even if one has all the water in the world, that does not mean that all our water based needs are fulfilled.
